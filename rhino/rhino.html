Shaifali Parashar

I am a CNRS Research scientist at LIRIS, INSA-Lyon. Previously, I was a postdoc researcher at CVLAB, EPFL working on 3D computer vision with Prof. Pascal Fua and Dr. Mathieu Salzmann. I received my PhD in Computer Vision at University of Clermont-Auvergne, France in 2017. I was supervised by Prof. Adrien Bartoli and Dr. Daniel Pizarro. My research interests are non-rigid 3D reconstruction, deformable SLAM and geometric shape processing.
